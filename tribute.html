Lord of the Rings

Frodo Baggins is a fictional character in J. R. R. Tolkien's writings, and one of the protagonists in The Lord of the Rings. The Lord of the Rings is a series of three epic fantasy adventure films directed by Peter Jackson, based on the novel written by J. R. R. Tolkien. The films are subtitled The Fellowship of the Ring (2001), The Two Towers (2002), and The Return of the King (2003). Produced and distributed by New Line Cinema with the co-production of WingNut Films, the series is an international venture between New Zealand and the United States. The films feature an ensemble cast including Elijah Wood, Ian McKellen, Liv Tyler, Viggo Mortensen, Sean Astin, Cate Blanchett, John Rhys-Davies, Christopher Lee, Billy Boyd, Dominic Monaghan, Orlando Bloom, Hugo Weaving, Andy Serkis and Sean Bean. Set in the fictional world of Middle-earth, the films follow the hobbit Frodo Baggins as he and the Fellowship embark on a quest to destroy the One Ring, to ensure the destruction of its maker, the Dark Lord Sauron. The Fellowship eventually splits up and Frodo continues the quest with his loyal companion Sam and the treacherous Gollum. Meanwhile, Aragorn, heir in exile to the throne of Gondor, along with Legolas, Gimli, Boromir, Merry, Pippin and the wizard Gandalf, unite to save the Free Peoples of Middle-earth from the forces of Sauron and rally them in the War of the Ring to aid Frodo by distracting Sauron's attention.

Timeline of Frodo Baggins in the Third-age

Year 2968

  • Birth of Frodo

Year 2980

  • Death of Frodo's parents, Drogo Baggins and Primula Brandybuck

Year 2989

  • Frodo is adopted by Bilbo Baggins and moves to Bag End around this time.

Year 3001

  • Frodo comes of age and inherits Bag End and the Ring from Bilbo.

Year 3018

  • 12 April: Gandalf arrives at Bag End.
  • 13 April: Frodo learns he has the One Ring and decides to leave the Shire.
  • 22 September: Frodo turns 50.
  • 23 September: Frodo leaves Bag End.
  • 25 September: Frodo learns of his friends' intention to accompany him.
  • 26 September: Frodo and his companions travel through the Old Forest and come to the House of Tom Bombadil.
  • 6 October: Frodo is wounded by the Witch-king at Weathertop. And awakens in Rivendell.
  • 25 October: At the Council of Elrond, Frodo volunteers to take the Ring to Mordor.
  • 25 December: Frodo and the Fellowship leave Rivendell.

Year 3019

  • 13 January: The Fellowship enters Moria.
  • 15 January: Gandalf battles the Balrog and falls into shadow. The Fellowship enters Lothlórien.
  • 29 February: Frodo meets Gollum and spares his life.
  • 1 March-2 March: Gollum leads the Hobbits through the Dead Marshes.
  • 5 March: The Hobbits reach the Black Gate and realize it's impassable. Frodo agrees to follow Gollum to a secret way into Mordor.
  • 24 March: The Hobbits reach the foot of Mount Doom.
  • 25 March: Frodo reaches the Cracks of Doom and claims the Ring for himself. Gollum bites off Frodo's finger and falls into the fiery chasm. The Ring is destroyed.
  • 15 July: Arwen tells Frodo that he may pass into the West in her stead.
  • 6 October: Frodo feels pain in his shoulder on the anniversary of his confrontation with the Witch-king.
  • 4 November: Frodo agrees to be Deputy Mayor.

Year 3020

  • 13 March: Frodo is ill on the anniversary of his encounter with Shelob.
  • Mid-year's Day: Frodo resigns as Deputy Mayor.
  • 6 October: Frodo is ill again.

Year 3021

  • 13 March: Frodo is ill again.
  • 29 September: Frodo sails into the West to the Undying Lands.
